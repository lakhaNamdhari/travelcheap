wo scenarios & two solutions The first thing you should ask yourself is what is the most important dimension for you: the height or the width of the available area in the browser? Scenario one: the height is more important Reasons could be you want your site to fit the screen without the need to scroll down (no vertical scrollbar) you want to be sure a particular image is visible (or text can be read) without using the scrollbar (but the scrollbar is available) Scenario two: the width is more important You want to fit your site nicely within the available width, and you don't care about the height because there's a scrollbar when needed.
